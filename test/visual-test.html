Each canvas shows a grid projected through the lead (blue) and follower (red) sampling matrices at different zoom levels. At focus depth D, the grids should overlap perfectly at the center. Off-center divergence = parallax (expected).

Blue = lead camera grid. Red = follower camera grid. Green dots = focus plane anchor points. At the image center, both grids should converge (same pixel ≈ same 3D point at depth D).

Left = warp OFF (prewarp crop only). Right = warp ON (homography correction). The warp version should show perspective-corrected alignment.
